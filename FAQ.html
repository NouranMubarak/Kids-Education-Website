Frequentely Asked Questions

Find the answers to the most common questions
about BrightStep Primary School.


What age do you accept?

We accept sudents from age Pre-K(age 4) through Grade 6. Each grade level
is designed to support your child's growth and development.

What are the school hours?

Our school day starts at 8:00 AM and ends at 2:30 PM,
Sunday through Thursday.

Do you offer school transportation?

Yes! We provide safe and reliable transportation services
for students in selected areas.

Do you provide meals or snacks?

Yes. Healthy snacks and balanced meals are available
to support our students throughout the day.

How can I enroll my child?

You can complete the online application form or visit
our admissions office with the required documents.

What is the tuition fee?

Tuition fees vary depending on the grade level.
Please contact our admissions team for detailed information.

Is BrightStep a safe environment for my child?

Absolutely! Student safety is our top priority. We provide a
secure campus, caring teachers, and a supportive learning environment.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! If you can't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to contact us directly.
